Onimusha Warlords: Where To Find the Rope Ladder | Descending Equipment

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

In this Onimusha Warlords guide, we explain where you can find the rope ladder, the equipment tool used to descend into the moat.

Recommended Videos

Playing Onimusha Warlords, there are several points where you will be asked to fulfill specific requirements. One of these moments is at the beginning of the game when we are asked to find something suitable for descending into the moat.

A rope ladder is undoubtedly suitable for this purpose and – guess what – you can easily find it in the initial stages of the game. In this guide, we explain how to find this equipment.

​

Where to Find the Rope Ladder

Previously, we have written a guide to some Trick Boxes you can find along the game. The rope ladder is linked to one of these challenges: you can find this tool in the first Trick Treasure Box.

  • First of all, look at the map to understand which is the exact starting point.

Then, solve this trick you have to:

  • Move the left square
  • Move the right square
  • Move the left square again

What can you find in the (other) Trick Boxes

  • A Rope Ladder
  • A Bow
  • A key that opens the blue doors in the area
  • A sacred knife
  • A great armor set
  • A decorated arrow
