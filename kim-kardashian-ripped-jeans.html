Kim Kardashian Pairs Shredded Jeans with a Croc-Embossed Corset

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

Kim Kardashian just debuted a new outfit that's a clear throwback to Y2K fashion.

On Instagram yesterday, the reality TV star and SKIMS mogul shared photos of her posing in extremely shredded jeans, with rips running down the entire length of her legs. The pants, courtesy of French designer Ludovic de Saint Sernin’s spring 2022 collection, also had lace-up details running down the sides and exposed pockets.

She completed the look with a lavender, croc-embossed corset with an asymmetrical silhouette, plus pointed cream-colored heels.

"Talk less and say more," she captioned the post.

Kardashian recently launched the SKIMS Icons campaign, which starred former Victoria's Secret Angels Tyra Banks, Heidi Klum, Alessandra Ambrosio, and Candice Swanepoel.

The modeling powerhouses reunited to pose in pieces from the shapewear brand's best-selling, size-inclusive Fits Everybody collection. Kardashian also shared photos of herself joining in on set and posing alongside the supermodels.

"OK so I wasn’t supposed to be in this @SKIMS campaign but I stopped by and jumped in because it was too iconic," she explained on Instagram. "Introducing Tyra, Heidi, Alessandra and Candice wearing our signature SKIMS Fits Everybody collection."

Earlier this week, Banks opened up to BAZAAR.com about choosing to star in the campaign due to her appreciation of the brand's mission to empower women of all body sizes. "My body is very different from my catwalk days, and I am excited to showcase it in all of its super-curvy glory," she said. "Ms. Kardashian, I did it for you, girl!"
