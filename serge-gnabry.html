Serge Gnabry Wikipedia

Gnabry was born in Stuttgart, Germany, to parents Birgit Gnabry and Jean-Hermann Gnabry. He was born on 14 July 1995 in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg. His father is of Ivorian and his mother is of German descent. From childhood, he was talented Sprinter but he choose Football over athletics.

Before returning to Germany and signing with Werder Bremen in 2016, he also spent a brief time on loan at West Bromwich Albion. After competing for Germany at various youth levels, David made his senior international debut in November 2016 against San Marino in a 2018 FIFA World Cup qualification game and scored three goals in an 8-0 victory.
