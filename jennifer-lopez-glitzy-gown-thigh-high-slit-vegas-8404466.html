Jennifer Lopez Wore a Glitzy Gown With the Highest of Slits in Vegas

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

When Jennifer Lopez gets dressed, she's on a mission to turn heads. Whether she's walking the red carpet with her gold underwear exposed underneath a sheer gown, or casually running errands in sky-high heels, J.Lo always goes for the glam. And when stepping out in Las Vegas, she turns it up even more. 

Case in point? On Friday, Lopez co-hosted a charity poker tournament in Vegas along with her husband, Ben Affleck, for his nonprofit Eastern Congo Initiative and she went all out with her style. For the occasion, J.Lo chose a daring cutout gown by David Koma that featured a sexy thigh-high slit and an asymmetric cutout stretching from her chest down to the opposite hip. The black dress also boasted a built-in choker that was covered in crystals, as was part of the bodice and hem. She carried a sparkly black clutch, and finished off her glitzy outfit with statement serpentine-shaped earrings and a smattering of silver rings. 

Lopez's caramel-highlighted hair was worn down in loose waves with a middle part, while the rest of her beauty look included nude lipgloss and a bronzy glow. 

Affleck, for his part, wore a bold blue suit with a white button-down underneath and black dress shoes.

Vegas, of course, is a special place for Bennifer as the couple tied the knot there in July 2022, eloping at the Little White Chapel. "We did it. Love is beautiful. Love is kind. And it turns out love is patient. Twenty years patient," Lopez previously wrote on her On the J.Lo newsletter, adding that their ceremony was "exactly what we wanted."

"In the end it was the best possible wedding we could have imagined," she continued. "One we dreamed of long ago and one made real (in the eyes of the state, Las Vegas, a pink convertible and one another) at very, very long last."
