Biography

Tom Paxton was born in Chicago, IL on October 31, 1937. Folk artist known for The Last Thing on My Mind, The Marvelous Toy, and other standards covered by numerous artists. He recorded albums with Chris Slade. He studied drama at the University of Oklahoma, during which time he fell in love with folk music after discovering the repertoire of Woody Guthrie and The Weavers.
