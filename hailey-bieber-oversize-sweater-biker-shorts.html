Hailey Bieber Layers the Coziest Oversize Knit Sweater Over a Cutout Bodysuit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Even on a casual coffee run, Hailey Bieber is still dressed to the nines.

The Rhode founder made a sporty chic fashion statement while out and about in Beverly Hills yesterday, accompanied by best friend Justine Skye.

Frankies Bikinis Clara Cutout Bodysuit

Bieber looked effortlessly cool in a nearly all-black ensemble, which had her layering an oversize ribbed-knit sweater over a bodysuit from Frankies Bikinis. The suit includes skin-baring details like spaghetti straps and an oval cutout underneath the bust, but her knit layer added a modest and cozy touch—perfect for sweater-weather season. She continued the sporty theme with a pair of chunky black sneakers.

To elevate the outfit, the model accessorized with her go-to tote bag—a boxy black leather purse from Saint Laurent—black oval sunglasses also from Saint Laurent, her signature bedazzled B necklace designed by Alex Moss, and thick gold hoop earrings. For a pop of color, she added a bright red baseball cap.

MEGA//Getty Images

Meanwhile, Skye showed out in her own all-black outfit, which consisted of a cropped bomber jacket, a low-rise midi slip skirt, glossy black Prada loafers, and a black purse.

MEGA//Getty Images

Bieber’s street style often features huge, oversize pieces layered over tiny bottoms.

Earlier this month, for instance, she headed out for a lunch date with husband Justin Bieber clad in a white oversize T-shirt that featured a black-and-white portrait of singer Frank Sinatra on a bright cyan background. Underneath the tee, the model wore a pleated black miniskirt.
