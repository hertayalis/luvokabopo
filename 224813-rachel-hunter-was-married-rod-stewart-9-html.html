Rachel Hunter's Husband Was Rod Stewart - Later She Started Dating a Hockey Player

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 1, 2024

Rachel Hunter was young when she married rock legend Rod Stewart. The musician acknowledged that their age gap, among other things, led to their separation. Years after their split, Hunter found a new love.

New Zealand model and actress Rachel Hunter tied the knot with Rod Stewart in 1990. She was 21 then, and the couple began expanding their family soon after. They welcomed two children, Renee and Liam Stewart.

They separated in 1999, with Hunter wanting out of their marriage. She filed for divorce in 2003 and later "dropped the proceedings." Her ex-husband filed his papers in 2005, and their divorce was finalized in late 2006. Hunter then moved on with an athlete.

Rod Stewart on Rachel Hunter Wanting a Divorce: 'It Knocked Me for Six'

Stewart touched briefly on his relationship with Hunter in a 2019 interview. The musician recalled his shock when Hunter expressed she wanted to separate. "I couldn't believe it. I was a rock star. You don't dump a rock star! It knocked me for six. It was a huge shock," he said.

However, he acknowledged that Hunter was young when they married, and her life changed drastically and quickly; she became a wife, and a year later, she was a mother. "My sister Mary told me she was too young for me as we were walking down the aisle. And she was right," he reflected.

Rachel Hunter and Rod Stewart during 1993 MTV Movie Awards at Sony Studios on June 7, 1993, in Culver City, California. | Source: Getty Images

Rachel Hunter Moved on with Ice Hockey Player Jarret Stoll — The Former Couple Almost Married

Shortly after her divorce from Stewart was finalized, Hunter found love with thirteen-years-younger athlete Jarret Stoll. The pair dated for nearly two years and shared a home in Los Angeles with Hunter's two kids.

News of their engagement broke in August 2008, with Hunter's rep saying she had "never been happier." "She has been with Jarret for a long time, and everyone is thrilled for them [...]. He is an amazing guy, and she is an amazing girl – they're truly meant to be," the rep gushed.

Rachel Hunter (R) and Jarret Stoll arrive at the launch party for Lyric Culture at the Avalon on May 10, 2007, in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

They planned to marry in August 2009, but Stoll reportedly called off their engagement without explanation. Rumors of their age gap and Hunter's "high-maintenance tastes" were said to have played a role in their separation.

However, she later addressed the matter, saying it was right for them to split, though admitting it was difficult for her. Stoll later moved on to sports newscaster Erin Andrews, and the pair married in 2017.

Rachel Hunter and Jarret Stoll attend the final game of the Anaheim Ducks/Minnesota Wild hockey playoff series at the Honda Center on April 19, 2007, in Anaheim, California. | Source: Getty Images

Rachel Hunter's Love Life Prediction from a Medium

In June 2023, Hunter appeared on "Hollywood Medium with Tyler Henry" and delved into her family trauma with the clairvoyant eponymous host. The pair briefly touched on Hunter's love life, with Henry sharing that he saw Hunter at her romantic "happiest" a few years down the line.
