Reactions as Tems wins her first Grammy award

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Nigerian singer, Temilade Openiyi, known professionally as Tems, has won her first Grammy award for her performance in Future’s ‘Wait For You’.

‘Wait For You’ won the “Best Melodic Rap Performance” category in the 65th Grammy Awards held at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles.
