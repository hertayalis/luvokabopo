Miley Cyrus &amp; Liam Hemsworth's Official Wedding Photos Have Been Shared on Instagram

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 10, 2024
  • Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth got married on December 23, 2018. Now, several family members have started sharing official wedding portraits on Instagram featuring the couple on their big day.
  • Miley's dad, Billy Ray Cyrus, shared several photos from the wedding, including one which he hilariously captioned, "Wedding wouldn't be complete without one shot from Dads out dated blackberry camera."
  • Hemsworth and Cyrus met prior to filming the movie The Last Song, and have been in an on-off relationship ever since.

Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth managed to pull off a secret wedding over the festive season, with fans of the couple only finding out on Instagram after the event.

Cyrus' dad, singer Billy Ray Cyrus, has now taken to Instagram to share photos from inside the wedding, which took place in the couple's home in Tennessee. Firstly, Miley's dad shared a sweet shot as he posed with his wife, and his newly married daughter writing, "Congratulations @mileycyrus and @liamhemsworth! Long live love!"
