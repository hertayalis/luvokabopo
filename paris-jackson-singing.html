Paris Jackson Singing - Michael Jackson's Daughter Paris Jackson Singing

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Paris Jackson proved she's inherited her dad's musical skills when she took the stage for a low-key gig over the weekend. The 19-year-old daughter of Michael Jackson shared the mic with her cousin, singer Austin Brown (Rebbie Jackson's son), to perform his song "Smile" in West Hollywood. The two alternated signing lines while Brown strummed the guitar and Jackson snapped along.

Listen below:

The teen has honored her dad in more ways than following his musical footsteps. On the eighth anniversary of his death in June this year, she posted a sweet tribute to him on Instagram and wrote in the caption: "My angel, my king, my universe. 8 years without you feels like a lifetime."

Paris isn't only harnessing her star power through singing; she's also signed with Kate Moss and Gisele's modeling agency and made her acting debut on Star earlier this year.

We'll be patiently waiting for her debut single.
