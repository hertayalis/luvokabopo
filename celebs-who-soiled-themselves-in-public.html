Celebs Who Soiled Themselves In Public

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 22, 2024

If you're not in the mood for a particularly graphic story of a grown man pooping his pants, you may want to skip over this one.

In 2011, Nick Kroll went on "Conan" and gave a full-on recap of the time a BM hit when he was still in his car. "I'm like, five minutes from my house, I am very close to home," he remembered. "So I'm like, 'I can do this.' And then I'm just like, [begins taking deep breaths], like butt Lamaze, because I'm about to have a butt baby, because I am crowning." The "Big Mouth" co-creator revealed he then had the idea to try to let a little out because he was about to blow, but that didn't go so well. "I hovering, holding onto my steering wheel for leverage ... and my body was like, 'No. We want it all.' So I'm sitting there ... and I just fill my underwear with, like, a travel-size pillow worth of brisket," he divulged. After the deed was done, Kroll waddled his way to the shower, before enjoying a post-poop nap following his traumatic experience.

The browning crowning story came back to haunt Kroll six years later though during a return to "Conan." The comedian was reminded of his antics after he claimed he didn't have anything in common with his "Captain Underpants" character, the aptly titled Professor Poopypants. Conan O'Brien knew better though, showing the previous interview in all its glory.
