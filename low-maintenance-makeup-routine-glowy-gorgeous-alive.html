My low-maintenance makeup routine takes me less than 2 minutes - I look glowy, gorgeous and alive

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 22, 2024

ROLLING out of bed and reporting for duty fully made up can be done in less than two minutes.

One TikTok user has shared her low-maintenance beauty routine.

The results are glowy, gorgeous and alive.

Just what Chloe Grayling (@lovechloejane) was aiming for.

This busy lady has one million followers on her platform.

“A quick low-maintenance routine for the days you’re running late, which for me it’s all of them," she said in her post.

Read more about makeup

She appeared bare-faced and was about to show how to do it.

“My daily makeup routine takes less than two minutes," she said.

She had an appropriate name for it.

“I call it the wake-up makeup," she wrote.

It might give you a few precious more minutes of snoozing.

“Honestly, you can roll out of bed do this makeup and look like you have got your life together," she wrote. “Which is the dream."

She concentrated her efforts on the basics.

“Most important thing is the brows, so we’re going to do them first," she said.

I have thick, course brows, but these four products always keep them in place, even in the wind and rain - and one only costs £3.99

Any patchy spots were filled in and brushed out.

"Fix them up with your fingers because they’re not perfect but nor are we," she said.

Some eyeliner came next, and it was a no-nonsense approach.

“We are going in with a super messy cat’s eye. But you know what, it's fine."

She was super-quick with the mascara, and didn't even bother with bottom lashes.

"Then we’re going to head in and do some concealer under our eyes to make it look like we’ve been sleeping and not lying away thinking about the state of the universe," she joked.

And then it was the final flourish in her two minute makeup job.

“And then a little bit of color on our cheeks is a vibe," she said.

“A vibe that makes you look glowy and gorgeous and alive. The dream."

She shook her hair out. "We're done," she said smiling widely."

Commenters were complimentary.

“This is my exact quick morning routine. Except I use bronzer too,” said one person.

“This is so stunning. Your skin is glowing. I will be doing this routine from now on," wrote a second person.
